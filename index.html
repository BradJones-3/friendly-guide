Jedi Mind Tricks

Welcome to Jedi Mind Tricks Memory Game!

The way to play Jedi Mind Tricks is to try and find all the matching pairs of cards before the timer runs out! If the cards do not match they will flip back around.

  • Padawan - 12 cards
  • Jedi Knight - 16 cards
  • Jedi Master - 20 cards
