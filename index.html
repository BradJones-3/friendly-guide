Welcome To Jedi Mind Tricks Memory Game That Puts Your Star Wars Knowledge To The Test!

Playing The Game

The objective of this game is to find all the matching card pairs in the least amount of moves, whilst working against a timer. If the cards don't make a pair then they will flip back over but if a player finds a matching pair then those selected cards will stay flipped over. You can also test your memory with three levels of difficulties.

  • Padawan - 12 cards
  • Jedi Knight - 16 cards
  • Jedi Master - 20 cards
